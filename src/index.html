The Order of the Phoenix is a secret society formed with a common goal: to aid in the defeat of Lord Voldemort.
It is apparently headed up by Professor Dumbledore, as it is he who directs the remaining members to reform the Order on Voldemort's return.
The headquarters for the order is 12, Grimmauld Place, London or "The ancestoral black house"
